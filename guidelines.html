Student Guidelines

  • Students should register by 11:59 PM, 10 April at the latest.
  • Students can choose a maximum of 5 companies of their interest in the registration form.
  • Students should update their forms(if required) by 11:59 PM, 10 April
  • Students must provide a link to their résumé in the registration form. (You can upload it on any cloud storage service and get a sharable link for it.)
  • Registered students will be notified about the schedule and the interviews will be offline Personal Interview. Therefore students are requested to frequently check these sources for updates.

Company Guidelines

  • Companies should register by 11:59 PM, 9 April at the latest.
  • The method of interview shall be offline Personal Interview.
  • Companies are required to mention the number of internship slots and the stipend in the registration form.
  • Companies will recieve the details of interested students (including their résumés) by 11 April.
  • The companies are required to send us the schedule of interviews of students by 12 April at the latest.
